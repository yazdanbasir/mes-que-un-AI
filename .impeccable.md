## Design Context

### Users
Single user (Yazdan) — personal Spanish learning app. Used daily in a focused reading/learning context. Not a commercial product; should feel personal and crafted, not enterprise SaaS.

### Product Structure
- **Articles tab**: Full article reader for pages retrieved from Spanish sports sites (Marca, AS, Sport, etc.)
- **Tweets tab**: Curated timeline from specified Spanish soccer accounts
- **Pau tab**: AI chatbot/conversational agent for language learning interaction

### Brand Personality
Modern, clean, professional, sleek, and warm. The warmth is the differentiator — everything else is clean and precise, but it should never feel cold or corporate.

3 words: **precise, warm, editorial**

### Aesthetic Direction
- Light mode. Warm cream/off-white background, not stark white.
- Anthropic-adjacent color palette: terracotta/clay orange as the primary accent, warm neutrals throughout.
- App shell card layout: sidebar + main content area, floating card centered on background.
- Should feel like a well-designed reading app — calm focus, high content legibility.
- References: Anthropic's own website aesthetic (warm, modern, typographically considered).

### Anti-References
- Basic, bland, old-school websites — flat hierarchies, default system fonts, generic layouts.
- Duolingo-style gamification aesthetics — bright, cartoonish, achievement-badge-heavy.
- Cold SaaS dashboards — blue-gray color palettes, everything feels like a utility tool.

### Design Principles
1. **Warmth through detail** — modern and sleek, but the warmth shows in color temperature, font choices, and breathing room, not in decorative flourishes.
2. **Reading is the core job** — typography and layout must serve long-form reading first. Articles are the heart of the app.
3. **Personal, not generic** — this is one person's tool, built around their specific interests. It should feel curated, not like an off-the-shelf product.
4. **Content over chrome** — UI elements should recede; content should lead.
5. **No AI tells** — no gradients on text, no glassmorphism, no hero metrics, no identical card grids.
